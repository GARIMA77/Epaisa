MOBILE POINT OF SALE APP

A simple, powerful and multi lingual point of sale that offers business management tools previously only accessible to the big box retailers.

Stay competitive, generate insights from sales data and sell anywhere, in the store, on the go or online.

SIMPLE POS Simple and powerful, the ePaisa POS is designed to run on any smartphone or tablet. You can manage stock, take an order, accept payments and send out digital receipts wherever your customers are. Integrate a receipt printer, cash drawer, barcode scanner and a tablet stand with your POS to get a complete point of sale for your store.

INVENTORY MANAGEMENT POS Get organized and create a great product library by adding, editing or removing products in the app in seconds. Create categories and speed up payments making it easier to keep track of all your sales. Get reorder alerts when you are running low on an item. Quickly scan in the product barcodes in your customer’s cart using a barcode scanner.

INTEGRATED PAYMENTS Accept all forms of payments – on your smartphone or tablet. Process all major credit cards and debit cards with the ePaisa wireless Chip & PIN reader and sell in-store & on-the-go. Send invoices to your customer’s email or text and accept online payments. Get paid with mobile wallets like Paytm, Mobikwik and Citrus Pay as well as cheques, loyalty points, bitcoins and more.

BUSINESS TOOLS Keep your customers happy by rewarding them with loyalty points. Drive repeat purchases with targeted SMS promotions and easily follow up on email. Get an overall view of your sales, inventory and traffic across multiple stores and gain actionable insights about your business. Get ideas for promotional offers based on top selling products and average spend. OFFLINE TO ONLINE

Get everything you need to set up your counter in store and sell online with our integrated Point of Sale. Easily handle all the hassles of selling online. Sync your products, inventory, orders, and customers across all online and hyperlocal marketplaces and accept payments and fulfill orders right from your ePaisa POS.
